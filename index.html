Frequently
Asked Questions

Do I need to be a professional host to host this trivia?

No! If you are personable and comfortable speaking in front of crowds you will be great hosting our trivia. Our included instructions on how to host trivia night will get you on the path to success!

How long does the game last?

Our games typically last between one hour and 15 minutes to one hour and 30 minutes.

Do we have to subscribe to the service?

The short answer is no. However, it will save you a lot of money if you do. Buying one-off custom trivias will cost significantly more in the long run.

Can I use the same trivia at multiple locations?

You will need to buy a subscription for each location you plan to run our trivia games at.
